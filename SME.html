SME (Small and Medium Enterprises)

The backbone economy of every developed nation lies on Small and Medium Enterprises. The need to create entrepreneurs who will in turn become employers in the future will help alot in the area of reducing unemployment in the state. That is why the state government takes it upon Itself to train and build employers thereby reducing unemployment, What A STRATEGIC PLAN!



The Beneficiaries were given an intensive one week training and Workshop on the fundamentals of a business. They were armed with the skills on how to successfully operate and manage a business. From theorical to hands-on session, they were fully equiped with skills like:

  • Cash Flow Management
  • Book Keeping
  • Generating Business Ideas
  • Writing and Implementing Business Plans
  • Building Business System, etc.

During the full one week of training, the participants were fed by the state government and mentorship programme was established to help check on the young entrepreneurs. The successfull participants were given some grants to help start up or boost thier businesses.
